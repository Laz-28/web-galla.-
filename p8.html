Lewis Hamilton

Lewis Hamilton is a British racing driver who competes in Formula 1 for Mercedes-AMG Petronas.

He is one of the most successful drivers in F1 history, holding records for most race wins, pole positions, and joint-most World Championships

Known for his aggressive driving style, consistency, and activism in diversity and sustainability.

Hamilton has been a dominant force in motorsport for over a decade.

He has won seven World Championships, equalling Michael Schumacher's record.

Hamilton is the first and only black driver to compete in F1
