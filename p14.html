Bugatti Chiron

The Bugatti Chiron is one of the most powerful, luxurious, and exclusive hypercars in the world.

It is powered by an 8.0-liter quad-turbocharged W16 engine that produces 1,479 horsepower and 1,180 lb-ft of torque.

Introduced in 2016 as the successor to the Bugatti Veyron, the Chiron pushes the boundaries of speed, technology, and engineering.

It has a top speed of 261 mph and can accelerate from 0-60 mph in just 2.5 seconds.

It is the fastest car in the world
