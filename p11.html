BMW M5 CS

The BMW M5 is the fastest vehicle produced by BMW

It is a high performance variant of the BMW 5 Series executive car built by the Motorsport division of BMW.

This car can reach speeds of upto 333km/h, has a power of 635hp, 4.4L engine and can hit 60 mile in 3 seconds

It is the first M5 to have a carbon fibre roof, which reduces the weight of the car

It' one of the most affordable high speed cars
