Enduro

An enduro bike is a mountain bike designed for downhill racing and technical terrain.

They are versatile enough to climb well, but their main focus is descending.

Suspension: Enduro bikes have longer front and rear suspension.

Brakes: Enduro bikes have larger rotors and more powerful pads.

Geometry: Enduro bikes have a slack head tube angle and longer wheelbase than trail bikes.

Weight: Enduro bikes are often heavier to handle technical descents.
